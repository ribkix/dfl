Bot Rules

If you don't follow the following rules, your uploaded bot will be declined.

  • The uploaded bot must be created by you.
  • The uploaded bot must abide with the Discord Terms of Service.
  • The uploaded bot must not include spam in the description.
  • The uploaded bot must not include any NSFW content (includes the icon and the description).
  • All NSFW commands must only function in NSFW channels.
  • The prefix must be the actual prefix.

Extra notes:

  • If the bot is offline while testing, we will contact you and your bot will still be checked in a later time.
  • If your bot is not checked within the 3 days you will contact you with the reason why it's taking longer than usual.
